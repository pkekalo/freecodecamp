Thanksgiving

The Celebration in Canada and USA

        Today the most people think of Thanksgiving as a day where people eat turkey, many other side dishes and do many other activities with our families. That is all good, but what is the history behind it? Well we do all of these things today, but what did the pilgrims English settlers of New England do many years ago? They didn't have a lot of food, games American football, Thanksgiving Bingo, Turkey Tag, Turkey Hunt, or most of the things we have and do today. Most people curious about Thanksgiving and kids ask their parents what is that all about and why is it a day where people all care that much. Let's take a look inside of American history to answer common questions and curiosities.
